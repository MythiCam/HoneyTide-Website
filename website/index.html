Stewardship, Education, and a Passion for Bees

```

HoneyTide Apiary is a small Oregon apiary dedicated to sustainable beekeeping, pollinator stewardship, and education. What began as a fascination with honey bees has grown into a journey of learning, conservation, and community connection.

Every colony, swarm capture, inspection, and lesson helps shape the future of HoneyTide. We believe healthy pollinators are essential to healthy ecosystems, thriving agriculture, and vibrant communities.

```
```

What We Do

Sustainable Beekeeping

Managing honey bee colonies with a focus on long-term health, responsible stewardship, and ethical beekeeping practices.

Pollinator Education

Sharing knowledge about honey bees, native pollinators, habitat creation, and the important role pollinators play in our environment.

Swarm Assistance

Helping relocate honey bee swarms whenever possible and educating the community about their importance.

```
```

Growing Through Education

HoneyTide Apiary is actively involved in continuing education through the Oregon State University Master Beekeeper Program, Bee Advocate initiatives, and participation in the Lane County Beekeepers Association.

Beekeeping is a lifelong learning process, and HoneyTide is committed to sharing that journey openly with the community.

```
```

Follow Our Journey

From establishing our first colonies to capturing swarms and documenting seasonal growth, HoneyTide is building an apiary one season at a time.

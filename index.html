A Small Oregon Apiary Growing with Purpose

```

HoneyTide Apiary is a small, developing apiary rooted in sustainable beekeeping, pollinator stewardship, education, and a deep respect for the natural rhythms that support healthy colonies and thriving ecosystems.

What began as a personal fascination with honey bees has become a hands-on journey of learning, care, conservation, and community connection.

HoneyTide is being built one colony, one swarm, one inspection, and one season at a time — with patience, curiosity, and a commitment to doing the work responsibly.
```

What HoneyTide Focuses On

```

Sustainable Beekeeping

Caring for honey bee colonies with attention to colony health, responsible management, seasonal needs, and long-term resilience.

Pollinator Stewardship

Supporting awareness of honey bees, native pollinators, habitat, forage, and the connection between pollinator health and healthy communities.

Apiary Education

Sharing the learning process openly through beekeeping education, community resources, and real updates from the apiary.

Swarm Assistance

Helping with honey bee swarm relocation when possible and offering calm, informed guidance to community members who encounter swarms.

```

Growing Through Education

```

HoneyTide Apiary is actively growing through continued education, including the Oregon State University Master Beekeeper Program, Bee Advocate work, and participation in the Lane County Beekeepers Association.

Beekeeping is a lifelong learning process. HoneyTide is committed to documenting that journey honestly — including the successes, the lessons, and the seasonal decisions that shape a responsible apiary.
```

This is a static HTML/CSS site hosted on GitHub Pages for HoneyTide Apiary, a sole-proprietor beekeeping business owned and operated by Camron Purdum in the Springfield/Eugene, Oregon area.

The current issue is that the website sometimes sounds too institutional and says "HoneyTide Apiary" too often. The site should feel more personal, like visitors are meeting Camron and following the growth of his apiary.

Important corrections:
- Camron is enrolled in the OSU Master Beekeeper Apprentice Program.
- Camron participates in the OSU Bee Advocate Program.
- Camron is a Lane County Beekeepers Association member.
- Camron is personally listed on the Oregon State Beekeepers Association swarm list for Lane County.
- HoneyTide Apiary itself is not an official OSBA organization or service provider.
- Camron currently manages four colonies under HoneyTide Apiary: Zion, Tortooga, Butera, and The Anchor Colony.
- HoneyTide is being developed into a sustainable apiary that will eventually offer small-batch local honey, beeswax, and other hive products when colonies are strong enough to share surplus responsibly.
